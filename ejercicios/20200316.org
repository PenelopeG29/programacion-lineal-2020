1. Resuelve el problema:
   \begin{equation*}
    \begin{aligned}
   \text{Maximizar} \quad & -2x_{1}-x_{2}\\
   \text{sujeto a} \quad &
     \begin{aligned}
      -x_{1}+x_{2} &\leq -1\\
      -x_{1}-2x_{2} &\leq -2\\
       x_{2} &\leq 1\\
       x_{1}, x_{2} & \geq 0
     \end{aligned}
   \end{aligned}
   \end{equation*}
